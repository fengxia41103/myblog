This is, who I am

I'm having a lot of thoughts, of life, these days. I need to write these down because they are interesting, and one day when I lose my memory, I can read them and remember who I was. I don't expect anyone to see, to read, to care about what I write. I enjoy them. I'd like someday others will find these points/feelings/opinions, agreeable. But either way, I write what I feel, what I think, what I'd like to save for memory's sake. I want to remember, even many are better to forget.
