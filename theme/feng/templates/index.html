Building Prototype

I build prototypes. My mission is to lower the barrier for entrepreneurs to translate their idea into web application.

I specialize in building and delivering proof-of-concept (POC) using robust system architecture and technology. I believe many critical details can only surface when tried. An idea can look great on paper, but to get all its pieces working coherently together as one system is not an easy task. A prototype is an attempt to achieve such coherent integration. A working POC is exciting because it lowers risk, raises confidence, removes ambiguity, clarifies design, discovers barrier, and reveals a path to success.

Here I present some samples of my research works and recent projects (2015-16) — design documents, lesson learned, tools used, thoughts and discussions. Dig in and look around. If you'd like to convert your idea into a Django-based web application, I'm here to help.
