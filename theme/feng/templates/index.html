Introduction

Welcome to PY Consulting, a software development firm who aims to lower the barrier for entrepreneurs to enter the web application and mobile application arena.

We specialize in building and delivering prototypes using robust system architecture and proven technology. We believe many critical details and questions can only surface through trying. Getting a prototype is not an easy task, but a working POC is worth a thousand (and feel free to multiply a few 10s to it) words, because nothing is more convincing than seeing an idea in action.

These articles are contributed by our staffs, and more interestingly, some by our clients. Not only we have worked together on projects with success (and we have buried those failed ones and their owners in... no, we are not gonna tell you.), but hearing what they say about their industry and business, things that troubled them, and how we have approached with a solution, shed lights on how similar techniques can help you to find a way going forward.
